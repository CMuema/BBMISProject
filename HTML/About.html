Welcome to Pewa Damu

Pewa Damu is designed to streamline and simplify the process of blood donation and management. Our mission is to provide a reliable and efficient platform for donors, hospitals, and administrators to manage blood donations and transfusions effectively.

What We Offer

Our system includes a variety of features to support different user roles:

  • Donor Registration: Individuals can register as blood donors, providing essential information such as blood type, contact details, and medical history.
  • Donor Login: Registered donors can log in to update their information, view their donation history, and schedule new donations.
  • Administrator Access: Administrators have access to manage donor records, oversee blood inventory, and ensure compliance with health regulations.
  • Hospital Access: Hospitals can request blood supplies, track their inventory, and manage patient transfusions through our platform.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between blood donors and those in need of blood transfusions. By leveraging technology, we make it easier for donors to contribute to life-saving causes and for healthcare providers to ensure they have the necessary resources.
